Threadbot Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 11, 2026

Threadbot ("the app") lets you design custom printed products with AI and order them for manufacture and delivery. This policy describes what we collect, why, and your choices.

Information we collect

  • Account: your email address and sign-in identity (via Supabase authentication, including Google sign-in if you choose it).
  • Designs: the text prompts you write, images you upload as design references, the designs generated for you, and your saved-design library.
  • Feedback: ratings and notes you submit about generated designs.
  • Orders: when you purchase, your shipping name and address and order contents. Payment card details are collected and processed by Stripe — we never see or store your card number.
  • Usage: counts of design generations for plan limits, and technical logs for reliability and abuse prevention.

How we use it

  • To generate your designs: your prompts and reference images are sent to AI image and language model providers acting as our processors.
  • To manufacture and ship orders: order details, print files, and your shipping address are shared with our print-on-demand fulfillment partner.
  • To process payments and subscriptions: handled by Stripe.
  • To operate plan limits, prevent abuse, and improve reliability and design quality.

What we don't do

  • We do not sell your personal information.
  • We do not use your uploaded photos for anything other than fulfilling your design requests.

Data retention & deletion

Your account, saved designs, feedback, and usage records are kept while your account exists. You can permanently delete your account and this data at any time in the app: Settings → Legal & Data → Delete Account. Order records required for tax, accounting, and fulfillment are retained as required by law. You can also request deletion by email (below).

Processors we rely on

Supabase (authentication, database, storage), Stripe (payments), our print-on-demand fulfillment partner (manufacturing and shipping), and AI model providers (design generation). Each receives only the data needed for its role.

Children

Threadbot is not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect their data.

Changes & contact

We'll update this page when the policy changes. Questions or data requests: support@threadbot.app.
