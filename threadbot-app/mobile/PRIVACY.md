Art Inc Peoria ("we", "us") operates the Threadbot mobile app ("the App"). This policy explains
what we collect, why, and your choices.

## Information we collect
- **Account information** — your email address, used to create and secure your account
  (authentication is handled by Supabase).
- **Content you create** — the text prompts you enter, any reference images you upload, and the
  designs the App generates and you save to your Closet.
- **Order & shipping information** — when you place an order, your name and shipping address are
  collected to fulfill and ship your product.
- **Payment information** — payments are processed by **Stripe**. We do **not** receive or store
  your full card number; Stripe handles card data under its own policies.

## How we use information
- To provide the App's core features (generating, saving, and ordering designs).
- To process and ship orders you place (via our print partner, **Printful**).
- To operate, secure, and improve the service.

## Third parties we share with (only as needed to run the service)
- **Supabase** — authentication and storage of your account data and saved designs.
- **OpenAI** — your design prompts (and reference images) are sent to generate artwork.
- **Stripe** — payment processing.
- **Printful** — order fulfillment and shipping (receives your name and shipping address).

We do not sell your personal information.

## Data retention & deletion
We keep your account data and designs until you delete them or request account deletion. To
request deletion of your account and associated data, contact us at the address below.

## Children
The App is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect their data.

## Your choices
You can edit or delete your saved designs in the App, and request full account deletion by
contacting us.
